Artworks full of color, originality, passion, love, creativity and purityx !

I was a geek for Photoshop way before I ever picked up a camera. At that time, I was assisting a nationally recognized wedding photographer in action and was in awe with the entire experience. The way she commanded the shoot, the energy of being there for the right moment, the spectacular sight of great light, and the ability to put couples right in the midst of it all to make a jaw-dropping image… I was hooked and I never looked back.

Since then I have become the luckiest guy in the world. I get to work with clients all over the country and across the globe that inspire me to create beautiful images for them. And I have the freedom to explore my other passion, traveling the world. I’ve had my photos published in blogs and newspapers and my travel images published in international magazines. Both make me proud, but I always return home where receiving love for the images I create brings me the most joy.

Growing up, photography played a very important role in my family. My uncle Frank Moscoti Sr. and his son Frank, who assisted him, were successful photographers with a thriving business. Every holiday was nothing short of magical and as a child I was in awe with their ability to capture the entire experience in a series of images. Those days have since passed, but the memories live on forever in the photos that they created, which are now some of my most prized possessions.

It takes a lot of work to be great at photographing weddings. The intimacy of preparing for the ceremony, portraiture of the bride, groom, their bridal party, family and friends, the actual ceremony and reception... all shots need to be fun, unique, creative, beautiful.... and done fast! Being part of the party and capturing the celebration of a reception, it's a lot in one day, but I thrive on that energy. If I don't walk away from a wedding celebration exhausted, I have not done my job! And I always do.

Being in front of the camera is scary to many, but it doesn’t need to be. I come to my shoots to have fun. I want you to do the same. Come prepared and relax. It’s going to be beautiful!
